Our Story

Kenneth’s Milk Tea started as a small passion project right here in Barangay Lower Bicutan. Kenneth, a milk tea enthusiast since college, realized that our local community needed a place where students and workers could relax without traveling far or spending too much.

What began as a small pop-up stall has now grown into a beloved neighborhood spot. We brew our tea fresh daily, boil our pearls to the perfect chewiness, and serve every cup with a smile.
